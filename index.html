Uses



Local Government Use


Agenda Scanning
  • VoteScope is designed with local governments in mind
  • City Councils around the nation post meeting agendas on the web days before an upcoming meeting.
  • VoteScope scans these agendas and deploys polls on the topics outlined in them
  • VoteScope allows consituents to express their opinions on important topics to City Councils

Easy Deployment
  • The VoteScope app allows cities to choose which pre-generated polls to deploy.
  • Through the app, cities can see responses to these polls to help City Councils make descions.

Easy Polling
  • Constituents simply have to sign up under a city and can begin responding to polls deployed by the city.
